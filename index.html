A litter bit more about me

The camera I currently use is Nikon D810, with a 50mm f/1.8D lens for still life, 105mm f/2.8G for macro and portrait, and 20mm f/1.8G for landscape.

The software I use to edit the pictures is Lightroom and Photoshop in Windows, and GIMP in linux. By now, still life is my favorite, because I like playing with lights. I enjoy spend hours changing the lights in order to see the tiny different effect on my photo. I like clean and simple style, with perfect details.
